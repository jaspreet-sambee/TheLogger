What Changed
1
Tinted card backgrounds — Each card has a unique gradient tint matching its theme: red for weekly stats, gold for achievements, orange for streak, blue/teal for muscle groups. Replaces the flat identical dark cards.
2
Colored borders per card — Each card's border matches its tint color instead of all being red. Gives each section a distinct identity while keeping the palette warm.
3
Stat dots next to labels — Small colored dots beside "Volume", "Sets", etc. reinforce which color = which stat without making the numbers themselves rainbow.
4
Gradient-tinted streak dots — Calendar dots use two intensity levels (40% and 100% red) instead of flat grey, so worked-out days glow. Today gets a ring highlight.
